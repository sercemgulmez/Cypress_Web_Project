# Test Expansion Roadmap

## Week 1

- Stabilize homepage, search, search results, product listing, and product detail smoke tests.
- Review selectors against current public DOM.
- Keep anti-bot/security pages classified as manual-only boundaries.

## Week 2

- Expand category, filter, sorting, footer/legal, campaign, and mobile coverage.
- Keep interactions light and independent.
- Avoid live support, checkout, payment, account, or real form flows.

## Week 3

- Add accessibility smoke, SEO smoke, and cross-browser smoke.
- Introduce optional reporting and CI commands.
- Identify visual regression candidates without broad production screenshot crawling.

## Week 4

- Prepare staging-only scenarios for login, cart persistence, checkout, payment, address, saved card, and order confirmation.
- Define synthetic users, fake addresses, test payment methods, and reset strategy.
- Keep public production suite focused on safe smoke/regression checks.
